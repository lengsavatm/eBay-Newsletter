Seller Update | Winter 2024
Our Winter Seller Update brings you exciting features and enhancements to help you sell faster, more efficiently, and with greater peace of mind.
This winter, we’re rolling out new and enhanced features to help you reach more buyers, improve your sales, and protect your business.

To start, we're bringing new capabilities to your Active Listings page to help you expand your reach and sell more efficiently. eBay Store subscribers can now create sale events directly from the Active Listings page, and all sellers will benefit from new "sell it faster" recommendations.

To help you sell with greater peace of mind, we’ve also added enhanced security features for you and your employees, extended resolutions for unpaid items to seller-initiated offers, and increased the length of time your draft listings are saved.

Finally, as we continue to invest in our marketplace to ensure you have the resources you need to grow your business and increase buyer reach, increasing costs in the market have made it necessary for us to adjust our per-order fee.
